# Settings

## Profile

### Add your social networks

Currently supported:

- Twitter
- LinkedIn
- External website URL

Your socials will appear on your profile page and people will be able to click on them.

### Update account infos

Update your username, email address and bio

  Email is not publicly displayed and is only (for now) used for internal
  communication
